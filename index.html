Privacy Policy – MiComida Calorie Scanner

Last updated: June 2026

MiComida respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how MiComida collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

MiComida may process the following information:

  • Food barcode scans
  • Food photos submitted for AI analysis
  • Calorie tracking history
  • Daily calorie goals

AI Food Scan

When using the AI Food Scan feature, food photos selected or captured by the user are securely transmitted to OpenAI for image analysis and calorie estimation.

Images are processed only to provide food recognition and nutrition estimates inside the app.

MiComida does not sell uploaded photos or use them for advertising purposes.

AI-generated calorie estimates, portion sizes, confidence scores, and nutrition information may occasionally be inaccurate and should be used for informational purposes only.

MiComida is not intended to provide medical, nutritional, dietary, or health advice.

Camera and Media Access

MiComida may request access to the device camera and photo gallery in order to:

  • Scan food barcodes
  • Capture food photos for AI analysis
  • Select food images from the device gallery

Camera access is only used while the app is actively running and only after permission is granted by the user.

Users may revoke permissions at any time through Android device settings.

Data Storage

Food history, calorie goals, preferences, and cached food information are primarily stored locally on the user's device.

MiComida does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

Internet Access

Internet access is required for:

  • Barcode food lookup
  • AI food recognition

Third-Party Services

MiComida uses third-party services necessary for core functionality, including:

  • OpenAI – AI food recognition
  • OpenFoodFacts – barcode food database

These services may temporarily process submitted data in order to provide app functionality.

Accounts and Personal Data

MiComida does not currently require user account creation.

MiComida does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Children’s Privacy

MiComida is not directed toward children under 13 years old.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app.
