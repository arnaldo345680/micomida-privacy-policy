Privacy Policy – MiComida Calorie Scanner

Last updated: May 2026

MiComida respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how MiComida collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

MiComida may process the following information:

  • Food barcode scans
  • Food photos submitted for AI analysis
  • Calorie tracking history
  • Daily calorie goals

AI Food Scan

When using the AI Food Scan feature, food photos selected or captured by the user are securely transmitted to OpenAI for image analysis and calorie estimation.

Images are processed only to generate food recognition results inside the app. MiComida does not use food photos for advertising purposes.

AI-generated calorie estimates, portion sizes, confidence scores, and nutrition information may occasionally be inaccurate and should be used for general informational purposes only.

MiComida is not intended to provide medical, dietary, or health advice.

Local Storage

Food history, calorie goals, and app settings are stored locally on your device.

Accounts and Personal Data

MiComida does not currently require user account creation.

MiComida does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Camera and Media Access

Camera and media permissions are required to:

  • Scan food barcodes
  • Capture food photos for AI analysis
  • Select food images from the device gallery

The camera is only used while the app is open and active.

Internet Access

Internet access is required for:

  • Barcode food lookup
  • AI food recognition

Third-Party Services

MiComida may use third-party services including:

  • OpenAI
  • OpenFoodFacts

These services may process requests necessary for app functionality.

Children’s Privacy

MiComida is not directed toward children under 13 years old.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app.
