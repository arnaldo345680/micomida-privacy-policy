# Privacy Policy – MiComida - Calorie Scanner Last updated: May 2026 MiComida respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and stores information when you use MiComida. ## Information We Collect MiComida may process the following information: * Food barcode scans * Food photos submitted for AI analysis * Calorie tracking history * Daily calorie goals ## AI Food Scan When using the AI Food Scan feature, food images are securely sent to OpenAI for image analysis and calorie estimation. These images are used only to provide food recognition results inside the app. MiComida does not sell your data. ## Local Storage Food history, calorie goals, and app settings are stored locally on your device. MiComida does not currently require account creation. ## Camera Access Camera permission is required to: * scan food barcodes * take food photos for AI analysis The camera is only used while the app is open and active. ## Internet Access Internet access is required for: * barcode food lookup * AI food recognition ## Third-Party Services MiComida may use third-party services including: * OpenAI * OpenFoodFacts These services may process requests necessary for app functionality. ## Children’s Privacy MiComida is not directed toward children under 13 years old. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app. ## Contact For privacy questions or support, contact: techservicesaf@gmail.com
